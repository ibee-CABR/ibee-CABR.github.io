Institutional Profile

The National Building Energy & Environment Testing Institute (CABR-Energy & Environment) is a premier state-level third-party testing and calibration authority. We operate across 13 major laboratory bases nationwide, covering 43,000 square meters of specialized testing space. Our flagship facility in Tongzhou, Beijing, is one of China's largest integrated laboratories for building acoustics, optics, thermal environments, and renewable energy systems.

Key Technical Capabilities

Beyond standard testing, we provide advanced specialized services and global accreditation support:

Calibration Services

Precise calibration for instruments: Temperature, Wind Speed, Pressure, Particle Counters, and Dust Monitors.

Proficiency Testing (PT)

ISO/IEC 17043 accredited provider for HVAC components, air filters, and heat pump units worldwide.

The All-Climate Matrix

Field validation in Mohe (-53°C), Turpan (Dry-Heat), and Hainan (Tropical) for extreme environment reliability.

Academic Leadership
