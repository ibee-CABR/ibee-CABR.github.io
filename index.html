Biographical Sketch

Dr. Zhichao Wang is a distinguished Senior Researcher and PhD Supervisor at the China Academy of Building Research (CABR). He serves as the Director of the Environment and Energy Testing Research Center, overseeing a complex of 13 national-level laboratories.

With over two decades of experience, Dr. Wang's work has been pivotal in the large-scale implementation of clean energy solutions across Northern China, particularly in the field of Air Source Heat Pump (ASHP) technology and advanced ventilation optimization.
