Professional Summary

A Senior Researcher and PhD Supervisor with over 20 years of leadership in building environment control, HVAC systems, and building energy conservation. As a core leader at the China Academy of Building Research (CABR) and the State Key Laboratory of Building Safety and Built Environment, I chair research that directly informs China's energy policies and technical standards.

I am widely recognized for pioneering work in air filtration, ventilation systems, and the large-scale application of Air Source Heat Pump (ASHP) technology for clean heating in cold regions.

Standards & Professional Leadership

I have led the development and revision of over 30 National and Industry Standards in China, playing a foundational role in regulating air filtration, ventilation systems, and building energy efficiency.

  • Lead Editor (National Standards): Multiple key standards in HVAC and Environmental Testing.
  • International ISO Expert: Active member of ISO technical committees, bridging Chinese technical innovation with global standards.
  • Director: National Center for Quality Supervision and Test of Air Conditioning Equipment (since 2004).
  • Lead Editor of Books: "Design and Evaluation of Residential Ventilation" (China Architecture & Building Press) and "Temperature Measurement/Control Technology and Applications".
