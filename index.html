About Me

I am a Senior Researcher at the China Academy of Building Research (CABR), leading the National Building Energy & Environment Testing Institute. My career is dedicated to advancing the efficiency of HVAC systems, the integration of renewable energy in buildings, and the development of international standards for a sustainable built environment.

As a lead expert in ISO and ASHRAE technical committees, I bridge the gap between cutting-edge laboratory research and global industry standards.

Research Interests

  • Renewable Energy Integration: Solar-Assisted Ground-Source Heat Pump (SAGSHP) with seasonal energy storage.
  • Building Decarbonization: Zero-carbon heating solutions for cold regions and performance evaluation of high-efficiency heat pumps.
  • Indoor Environmental Quality: Advanced ventilation, residential kitchen exhaust optimization, and oily fume migration control.
  • Carbon Capture & Utilization: Physicochemical properties of binary amine-based deep eutectic solvents for building-scale carbon capture.
