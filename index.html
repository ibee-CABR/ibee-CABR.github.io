About the Institute

As the premier authority under the China Academy of Building Research (CABR), we operate 5 National-level testing centers specializing in HVAC, Solar Energy, Building Envelopes, and Energy Efficiency. In 2024, our Shunyi and Tongzhou laboratories expanded to support over 43 new international standards (ISO/ASHRAE).

National Testing Centers

HVAC Systems (NCTC)

Full-scale testing for Air Source Heat Pumps (ASHP), Chillers, and Fan Coil Units. Specialized in low-ambient COP validation.

Solar Energy

National Center for Quality Supervision of Solar Water Heaters (Beijing). Thermal performance & durability testing.

Building Envelope

Airtightness, thermal bridge analysis, and fenestration testing for high-performance facades.

Energy Efficiency

System-wide building energy auditing and IAQ (Indoor Air Quality) distribution mapping.
